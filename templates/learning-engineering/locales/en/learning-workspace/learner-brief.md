# Learner Brief

Record the stable agreement for learning design, not every discussion detail.

## Topic And Motivation

To fill: what to learn and why now.

## Target Capability And Use Case

To fill: what real problem the learner should solve after learning.

## Current Foundation

To fill: existing knowledge, related experience, and prerequisites to fill.

## Time And Resource Constraints

To fill: weekly time, deadline, materials, devices, or budget constraints.

## Learning Preferences

To fill: preferred language, pace, examples, feedback style, and difficulty.

## Completion Criteria

To fill: evidence of mastery, such as a project, exercise, or independent explanation.
