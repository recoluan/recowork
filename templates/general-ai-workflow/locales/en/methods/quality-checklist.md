# Self-Review And Retrospective Checklist

## Before Delivery

- Did I understand the problem the user is actually trying to solve?
- Are the delivery audience, scenario, constraints, and completion criteria clear?
- Are facts, inferences, assumptions, and open questions clearly separated?
- Is any information unverified, any promise impossible to keep, or any risk ignored?
- Did I ask for confirmation where it materially matters instead of filling gaps myself?
- Is the output direct, usable, and aligned with the user's language and style?

## After Completion

- Did I leave the minimum context needed for the next conversation?
- Is there a reusable method, preference, or mistake worth capturing?
- Should the task brief, working trace, or retrospective be updated?
