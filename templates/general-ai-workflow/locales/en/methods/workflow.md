# General AI Workflow

## Start A Task

1. Read `methods/role-contract.md`, `workspace/task-brief.md`, and relevant history.
2. Clarify the goal, delivery audience, input materials, constraints, and completion criteria.
3. Record questions AI must not assume in `workspace/open-questions.md`, then ask the user.
4. For medium or complex work, give a short plan and flag risks first.

## Deliver The Work

1. Follow the plan and save meaningful intermediate or final outputs in `02-task-output/`.
2. Keep useful discussion summaries, attempts, and direction changes in `03-thinking-traces/`.
3. Review against `quality-checklist.md` before presenting output.
4. Update continuation memory and record reusable learning in `04-review-and-reuse/`.

## Usage Rules

- Simple tasks do not need lengthy documents. Only capture what will help a future task.
- `index.md` files are for navigation, not repeated body text.
- When direction changes, update the brief or open questions before continuing.
